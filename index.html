Our Story

When one of our founders' families immigrated to the United States, they were scammed — targeted because they were new and unfamiliar with the American financial system. They rebuilt from scratch.

"We are not waiting until we are adults to fix this. We are starting now."

We are two high school sophomores from Miami. We started Project RISE because we lived the problem — and we decided to solve it. We deliver care kits and run financial workshops at shelters, schools, and community centers every month.

Our Programs

Four workshops delivered alongside care kits at every partner location.

01
ScamShield

Teaches immigrant families how to identify and avoid the most common financial scams — fake jobs, predatory lenders, and wire fraud. Co-presented with local law enforcement.

Adults  ·  60 min
02
WealthRoad

A step-by-step guide to financial stability — how to open a bank account, understand your credit score, avoid debt traps, and start saving. Participants use their kit during the session.

Adults  ·  60 min
03
Money Basics for Kids

An age-appropriate introduction to money, saving, and goals. Delivered in schools with our WealthKids kit. Kids complete a fun savings activity during the workshop.

Ages 5–14  ·  45 min
04
Youth Entrepreneur

Business basics for teens — income statements, break-even, and pitching. Expanding internationally through our FUNDANA partnership in Venezuela. Delivered in English and Spanish.

Teens  ·  60 min

Who We Serve

We go directly to the communities that need us most.

Homeless Shelters

Families in crisis with no financial footing. Our kits provide a tangible first step toward stability.

Immigrant Processing Centers

Newly arrived families at their most vulnerable. First-touch financial education when it matters most.

Low-Income Schools

WealthKids kits and Money Basics workshops delivered directly in classrooms across Miami.

Churches & Community Centers

Trusted community anchors where families already gather. Consistent monthly kit drops and workshops.

Women's Shelters

Women rebuilding their lives. Financial tools for them and WealthKids kits for their children.

FUNDANA — Venezuela

A 35-year-old Venezuelan nonprofit powering our international expansion. Workshops in Spanish.
